Air Handling Units (AHU)

Some time the AHU will be a packaged unit in the case the BMS will give an Enabled and a setpoint. You will get back lots of different value these will be input form the packed unit or additional sensor that have been added.

Dampers

Dampers have end switches these are an open closed signal that tell you in the damper is opened or closed. They break a lot, they also get set up wrong so always take them with a pinch of salt. They are normally controlled by and interlink in the control panel when the fan in enabled and the fan start once they are open.

Coils

Frost, Cooling, Heating can by Wet, Electric or Gas. Normal 0-10v.

  • If the off/after sensor of the coil is reading hotter then the intel side and the valve or % is 0 the valve will be LETTING BY (not closing properly).
  • If they are give a % and the temperature changes they are okay Gas and Electric and normal quicker to react the Wet, but they both have tendency to overshoot as they are hard to control.
  • Both gas and electric should never run if the fan is not working.

Thermal wheels

Some ahu have thermal wheels that can be used instead of frost coils I have always set them to 20 RPM but some time they are on VDS, I personal do see much point on running any slower or faster then 20 RPM but happy to discuss.

Fans (When is a fan running?)

  • The fan is enabled and the DPS is made (true or 1) or flow sensor has a positive number it is running.
  • The fan is disable and the DPS is made (true or 1) or flow sensor has a positive number it is running.
  • The fan is disable and the DPS is made (true or 1) or flow sensor has a positive number, but the off-coil temperature is really high (close to the feed/flow temperature of the CT) the fan is NOT running.
  • The fan is enabled and the DPS is made (true or 1) or flow sensor has a positive number but the off-coil temperature is really high (close to the feed/flow temperature) the fan is NOT running.
