Variable Drive Speeds (VDS)

With VDS (variable drive speeds) or invertors as they are sometimes called, the enable means very little it just mean start but it could be started a 0%/0volts.

In the VDS there will be a max Hz set the will equal 100% 10volts this could be 50hz (or the max rating of the equipment or more then likely a reduce value set be they air balancing as a min and max for air quality.

If VDS is running 100% for a long time:

  • That there are issues onsite with open duct work.
  • The AHU (air handling unit) is under sized this can be that item have change or add since build/fit out.
  • Motor or blades of the fan are failing, and it has to run faster to achieve the same state.

At 100% for long periods this point the VDS is adding more losses due to inefficiencies, which can be as much as 10%.
