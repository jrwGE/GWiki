Fans (When is a fan running?)

  • The fan is enabled and the DPS is made (true or 1) or flow sensor has a positive number it is running.
  • The fan is disable and the DPS is made (true or 1) or flow sensor has a positive number it is running.
  • The fan is disable and the DPS is made (true or 1) or flow sensor has a positive number, but the off-coil temperature is really high (close to the feed/flow temperature of the CT) the fan is NOT running.
  • The fan is enabled and the DPS is made (true or 1) or flow sensor has a positive number but the off-coil temperature is really high (close to the feed/flow temperature) the fan is NOT running.
