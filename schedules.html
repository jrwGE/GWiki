Time Schedules and OSS

If the builds state open hours of 7 til 7 m-f but the plant run out of this range, this may be OSS or that the control TS is not at the same time. As the state time on edge.

Optimised Start Stop (OSS)

In Trend the default OSS has a warmup is 360 min this is 6 hours. The OSS if the OAT is 15⁰C and the office space is 20⁰C with a SP of 21⁰C the OSS will send a warmup run command to start all the plant connect an amount of time before the time schedule stated time.

You can also have cooldown so if the SP is 21⁰C and the space temp is 22⁰C and the OSS has worked out that if the TS turn off at 5pm and that the building stays above 21⁰C for an hour after 5pm the off will ask for the equip turn of an hour before 5pm.
